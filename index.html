Meet the PicoBoyAdvanced Console

The PicoBoyAdvanced is a cutting-edge retro gaming console built on the RP2040 microcontroller. Featuring a high-resolution OLED display and precision controls, it's designed for both developers and gamers.


Key Features:

  • RP2040 Microcontroller
  • High-resolution OLED Display
  • Premium Gaming Controls
  • Customizable Firmware
  • Modern Audio System
